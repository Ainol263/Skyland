Semeling Street Art
Address : No. 17, Jalan Raya Semeling, Kampung Mata Hari Naik, 08100 Bedong, Kedah


There are 95 drawings of cartoon characters, scenery, and Kedah history, as well as 3D drawings, that decorate the once-quiet back street of Semeling town. According to locals, the increase of visitors to the 'cowboy town' area helped to expand Semeling while also boosting the local economy. Tourists claim that the painting isn't particularly nice, yet it is the work of a Kedah resident.

Why would there be Kedah volunteers? This is because the project aims to promote the activities of Kedah residents rather than relying on the services of outside artists. A total of 40 artists from Kulim, Sungai Petani, Alor Setar, and other parts of the state contributed to the creation of 95 paintings, with the help of 300 volunteers, including students.
