Andalusia Mosque, Bandar Laguna Merbok
Address : Masjid Andalusia, Bandar Laguna Merbok, 08000 Merbok


The mosque is a house of worship for Muslims in performing prayers and religious ceremonies as well as the center of social activities for Muslims. The mosque is an institution of worship that should grow in line with the current developments and play a role in the life of the Muslim community as an institution to build the progress of the ummah. Enraging the mosque is a task that is closely related to the concept of human creation to implement the commandments of good and evil.

Therefore, every Muslim individual should show commitment to ensure that the management of the mosque functions as a center of worship and development of the ummah. In addition, the administration and management of the mosque need to be updated, especially the mechanism that is characterized by professionalism in the entire management and administration system of the mosque. This is because the development and excellence of the ummah start from the extent to which the movers are effective in implementing the management of the mosque.

Masjid Andalusia in Bandar Laguna Merbok is one of Sungai Petani, Kedah's most important mosques. The mosque's position in Sungai Petani, which is home to a number of tourist attractions including Bujang Valley, The Carnivall Waterpark, and Gunung Jerai, making it a perfect spot for people to stop by and admire the mosque's magnificence.

The mosque was constructed on 1.32 acres of waqf land. The mosque has a maximum capacity of 3,200 people. Phase 1 of this mosque's construction began on June 28, 2011, at a cost of RM2.95 million. The mosque's first phase was completed in December 2012, and the inaugural prayer was held on Friday, July 5, 2013.
