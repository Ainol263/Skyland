Complex Jetty Semeling
Address : Pusat Interpretasi Pelancongan Kuala Muda, Kompleks Jeti Semeling, Sungai Merbok, 08000 Sungai Petani, Kedah



Sungai Merbok Recreation Park is a fun and interesting site to visit in Sungai Petani. It's in the Semeling Jetty Complex, which is around 7 kilometers from the city center. The Merbok Mangrove Forest Reserve Biodiversity includes this leisure park. The Mangrove Forest Reserve is the biggest attraction here. A boardwalk called the Mangrove Platform allows visitors to view the mangrove trees that grow alongside the river.
Visiting Hours- Everyday 8.00 am - 5.00 pm

Built-in 2006, the Semeling Jetty Complex and Sungai Merbok Recreational Park were finished in 2009. A jetty, shop space, multipurpose hall, auditorium, and other amenities are available. The entire region is 12 acres and includes both land and marshes. In fact, the Semeling Jetty Complex or Sungai Merbok is the Kuala Muda District Tourism Interpretation Center. Visitors can get information on tourism products available in the district before visiting it.

Sungai Merbok Recreation Park is located on the outskirts of Sungai Petani. The location is next to the Semeling Jetty Recreation Complex. In English it is named Sungai Merbok Jetty Complex. Don't ask because I'm confused. The distance is about 7KM from the city center. From the Sungai Petani Selatan exit of the PLUS highway, the distance is only 6 KM.
